Callie Rose Petal (born 17 April 1994) is a Scottish conceptual artist, composer, software developer, and language artist whose praxis spans experimental literature, noise music, durational work, ritual installation, visual art, and immersive transmedia world building. She is known for her work as a noise alchemist, blending experimental sound, ritual structure, and semiotics in an artifact-as-artefact approach—treating distortion and formal degradation as recursive sites of meaning. Her visual, durational and sonic practice engages breakdown not as flaw, but as compositional force — paralleling her broader conceptual framework, in which loss, grief, and otherness emerge as structural generators of affect and form. Her work is often informed by her lived experience as a disabled trans woman and trauma survivor.
