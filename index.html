Callie Rose Petal''' (born 17 April 1994) is a Scottish conceptual performance artist, composer, software developer, and language artist whose work spans experimental literature, noise composition, and transmedia ritual. She is known for her work as a noise alchemist, blending experimental sound, ritual structure, and semiotics in an artifact-as-artefact approach—treating distortion and formal degradation as sites of meaning. Her sonic practice engages breakdown not as flaw, but as compositional force — paralleling her broader conceptual framework, in which loss, grief, and otherness emerge as structural generators of affect and form. Her work is often informed by her lived experience as a trans artist.
