Grimoire I: Ingestion
The Descent
back to gallery

One of two grimoires documenting Petal's descent into hypergraphia -- an emergent trait of her psychosis following assault -- the pages scattered with calligraphy and mysterious symbols are a harrowing insight into a mind in the throes of a psychotic break. Ingestion was created over a period of months leading up to the artist's admission to hospital. It is one hundred and sixty five pages long.
